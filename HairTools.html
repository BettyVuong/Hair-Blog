Introduction

Let's discuss some hairstylists must haves and classics. There's a reason certain products are used by hairstylists universally. I'm going to talk about some hot tools, brushes, and accessories that are a holy grail by the industry. Typically you can find these products on Amazon, Sephora, Chatters, Walmart, Shoppers Drug Mart, May&Max, your local beauty supply store, and more! Since I have a license, I could purchase some licensed exclusives at CosmoProf but I'd encourage you to talk to your hairstylist, they'll likely be selling the thing you're looking for or something similar.

Curling Iron - Hot Tools

Hot Tools is a popular brand for hairstylists and their preferance for curling irons. The Signature Gold series is not too expensive, durable, and high quality. The curling irons doesn't snag your hair, heats up fast, and the spring it smooth. I'd recommend the 1-1/4" barrel since it's a versatile curl.

Find the Hot Tools 1-1/4" curiling iron on Amazon for $60.50

Curling Iron - BabylissPRO

BabylissPRO is another brand that's popular to hairstylists for curling irons. The performance is similar to Hot Tools. The difference here is that the clamp is removable which is great for those who prefer to wrap their hair around the wand instead of using the clamp and twisting the barrel.

Find the BabylissPro Nano 1-1/4" curiling iron on Amazon for $77.99

Hair Dryer - Dyson

The Dyson Supersonic Hair Dryer is in it's own league on the market and has been for almost a decade. The technology makes the hair dryer compact which makes your arms feel more balanced when drying your hair. The air flow is strong compared to others on the market which allows for a quick dry. There are three heat settings, a cold shot, and three speed settings The attachments are high quality as well.

Find the Dyson Supersonic Hair Dryer at Sephora for $579.99

Hair Brush - The Wet Brush

What is so different about this hairbrush? Let me tell you, everyone I've given a Wet Brush to has joined some secret fan club for this product. A fellow hairstylist tried and can confirm it's the best on the market. The bristles are a patented design that's soft and flexible, which does not as much as a normal brush does. This brush lets you safely brush your wet hair without worrying about hair breakage. No more ripping out your hair or having a mental fight with your hair brush!

Find The Wet Brush on Amazon for $14.99
